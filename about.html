Why Gym Stars


About us:


Gym Stars is a small gym in Amsterdam that focuses on lifestyle coaching with a holistic approach. We believe that leading a happy life does not come by chance. Maintaining a healthy body and mind is essential - it's a necessity for anyone who wants to live positively every day.

At Gym Stars, we don't just focus on losing weight or working out. We focus on the root of happiness, or feeling good: regaining control, feeling good about yourself, building discipline, feeling accomplished, feeling confident, feeling attractive, having the right hormonal and chemical balance in your body.
